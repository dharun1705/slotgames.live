Contact Us

We welcome you to 6club game contact page. This is the place where you can talk to us anytime you need.

We know sometimes players have questions. Some players may need help with their game. Some of them want to ask about their winnings. Some may simply wish to give us ideas and strategies.

That is why we are always ready to listen.

Why You Should Contact Us

We’re always happy to hear from you and help in any way we can. You might want to get in touch with us for many different reasons, such as:

  • You’re unsure how a game works or need help getting started.
  • You’re facing an issue while playing
  • You have questions about our game results
  • You want to ask about deposits, withdrawals, or payments
  • You believe there may be an error in your result
  • You have a suggestion or a new idea you’d like to share
  • You’re not satisfied with something and want to talk about it
  • Or you simply want to share your experience with us

No concern is too small or unimportant. If something is on your mind, feel free to reach out to us anytime — we’re here to listen and support you.

How You Can Reach Us

Getting in touch with our team is simple and convenient. If you need any help or have a question, you can contact us by email.

Our email address: info@6clubgames.com

When writing to us, please include your name and game ID in the message. Sharing these details helps us locate your account quickly and respond to you as soon as possible.

What Happens After You Send An Email

Once we receive your email, our team carefully reads your message to fully understand your concern.

We then review the details from our side and work together to find the right information or solution for you. Every message is handled with care and attention.

After that, we get back to you with a clear and simple reply. We explain things step by step so everything is easy to understand. Our aim is to make sure you feel comfortable, informed, and satisfied after hearing from us.

Time Needed For Reply

We understand that your time is valuable, so we do our best to respond as quickly as possible.

At times, we may receive a high volume of emails, which can cause slight delays. But rest assured, every message is carefully read, and no email is ever ignored.

Tips To Get Quick Help

  • Always include your player ID or username.
  • Tell us clearly what happened
  • Try to explain your question in simple words
  • If possible, share screenshots of the problem
  • Use the same email that you used to register on 6 club

These steps will help us solve your issue faster and more easily.

Your Words Are Important To Us

Every message you send is important to us. Your feedback helps us to make 6 Club better and more fun.

Many good changes in our game have come from players like you. If you have ideas or wishes, please feel free to tell us.
