Privacy Policy

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the official 6 Club website, accessible at https://6clubgames.com. We value the trust you place in us and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This page explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect Through Comments

When visitors leave comments on our website 6club we collect the information shown in the comment form, along with the visitor’s IP address and browser details. This helps us prevent spam, abuse, and unauthorized activity.

An anonymized string created from your email address may be shared with the Gravatar service to check whether you are using it. The Gravatar service privacy policy is available at https://automattic.com/privacy/. Once your comment is approved, your profile picture may appear publicly next to your comment.

Media Uploads

If you upload images to the website, we recommend removing any embedded location data (EXIF GPS). Other visitors may be able to download images from the site and view this information.

Uploading media without sensitive data helps protect your privacy and personal safety.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies help improve your experience on our website. If you leave a comment, you may choose to save your name, email address, and website in cookies so you do not have to re-enter them next time. These cookies remain active for one year.

When you visit our login page, a temporary cookie is set to check whether your browser accepts cookies. This cookie does not store personal information and is deleted when you close your browser.

Login cookies last for two days, while screen preference cookies last for one year. If you select “Remember Me,” your login will stay active for two weeks. Logging out removes these cookies.

If you edit or publish an article, a cookie is saved to store the post ID. This cookie does not contain personal data and expires after one day.

Embedded Content From Other Websites

Articles on this website may include embedded content such as videos, images, or links. Embedded content behaves in the same way as if you visited the external website directly.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with their content, especially if you are logged in to their platform.

Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell, trade, or misuse your personal data. If you request a password reset, your IP address may be included in the reset email to protect your account from unauthorized access.

How Long We Retain Your Data

Comments and their related information are stored indefinitely. This allows us to recognize and approve follow-up comments automatically.

For users who register on our website, personal information is stored in the user profile. Users can view, update, or delete their information at any time, except for usernames which cannot be changed.

Your Rights Over Your Data

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. You may also ask us to delete your personal data.

This does not include data we are required to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where Your Data Is Sent

Visitor comments may be checked through automated spam detection services to maintain the safety and integrity of our platform.
